About Pixel Systems

Pixel Systems is a leading IT solution provider based in Pune, Maharashtra, with a rich history spanning over two decades. Established in 2005 and operating under the name 'Pixel' since 1999, we have consistently delivered excellence in computer maintenance and IT services across Maharashtra.

Our team comprises skilled IT professionals with extensive industry experience, united by a common goal to advance IT services and establish Pixel Systems as the leading IT solution provider across India. We specialize in comprehensive IT support, from basic maintenance to complex enterprise solutions.

Our vision is to be the leader in IT services by enabling our business partners to achieve long-term success through our innovative and comprehensive IT solutions. We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients by providing reliable, cost-effective, and cutting-edge technology solutions.
